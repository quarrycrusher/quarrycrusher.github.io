HCS cone crusher

HCS cone crusher, with the full name HCS series hydraulic cylinder cone crusher, is a new and efficient crusher based on our twenty years' experience in the crushing industry. HCS hydraulic cylinder cone crusher collects mechanical, hydraulic, electrical, automation, intelligent control technology into one set, and represents the world's most advanced crushing technology. It can be widely used in the medium crushing, fine crushing and super fine crushing operations.

HCS Cone Crusher Working Principle

The motor drive belt wheel, transmission shaft do periodic rotating motion. the material into the crushing chamber to have crushing, grinding. until crushing materials qualified, then discharged from the discharging port.Hydraulic system have very good insurance effect while greatly reduce the maintenance rate and improve production efficiency.

HCS cone crusher Distinct Benefits and advantages

  1. Stable operation and easy maintenance;
  2. High production capacity and excellent grain type of output product;
  3. The crushing chamber uses "V"-shaped structure, make it easy to control and adjust accurately;
  4. Using positive pressure dustproof system to prevent dust from polluting oil, damaging bearings, to extend the life time of oil and equipment;
